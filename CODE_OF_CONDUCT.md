# CodeVelo community code of conduct

CodeVelo wants its public repositories to be useful, professional, and welcoming.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate respectfully and assume good faith.
- Keep discussion relevant to the repository and the work at hand.
- Give actionable technical feedback without attacking people.
- Respect privacy, confidentiality, and intellectual-property boundaries.
- Disclose conflicts of interest that could affect a contribution or review.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, publication of private
information, malicious security activity, and repeated bad-faith participation are not
permitted.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove content and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when necessary to protect the community or project. Serious or
sensitive concerns may be reported privately to
[hello@codevelo.dev](mailto:hello@codevelo.dev).
